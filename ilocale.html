iLocale

Project Link: Github Repo

Please see Github repo above for more details.

iLocale is a web application featured in Carlos Adrian's portfolio, designed to offer users a seamless experience in discovering businesses across the globe. By harnessing the power of location-based technology, iLocale enables users to explore a diverse range of establishments, including restaurants, hotels, fitness centers, and more, no matter where they are in the world.

Beyond business listings, iLocale empowers users with valuable insights through user reviews and comprehensive business information. With a user-friendly interface, visitors can easily access essential details about a specific business, making informed decisions and enhancing their overall experience.

With iLocale, Carlos Adrian aimed to create a platform that not only simplifies the process of finding businesses but also fosters a sense of community by encouraging users to share their opinions and experiences. By providing a valuable resource for travelers, locals, and business enthusiasts alike, Carlos Adrian aspires to contribute to enhancing the way people explore and connect with businesses worldwide.

Features:

  • Google O Auth Integration with Passport.js
  • Basic Authentication with Passport.js
  • Email Sending with Nodemailer
  • Geocode locations with Mapbox API
  • Cluster Geocoded locations with Mapbox API
  • Upload Images and Store in Cloudinary
  • Authorization (Superuser role, Admin role, and Regular user role)
  • Paginate with Fetch API

Functions:

  • Authenticate users with Google or by signing in with their created account
  • Authorization and User management. Superusers can change the role of a specific user to admin and manage other users' posts
  • Authorization. Superuser and admins can create, edit, and delete categories
  • Authenticated users can add, edit, and delete businesses
  • Authenticated users can comment on different users' posts
  • Authorization. Only users who created the post and review can modify or delete them
  • View clustered businesses through Mapbox
  • Image Upload (Maximum of 5 Images). After posting a new business to the app, images will be uploaded to Cloudinary
  • Geocode locations. Pin locations on maps after posting a new business
  • Send emails through a contact form
  • Paginate businesses, reviews, categories, and users with Fetch API
  • Search businesses through their name or location
  • Sort businesses by most reviews, most recent, or oldest
