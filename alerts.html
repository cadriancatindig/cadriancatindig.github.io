Non-Prod to Production Application Support - Alerts Triaging

One of the essential daily responsibilities of a DevOps Engineer is providing support for the applications under their care. I have been assigned a task involving triaging alerting errors received via email and performing in-depth root cause analysis to identify the source of these errors. This task holds significant importance for the smooth operation of the company.

In this task, it is imperative to deliver a comprehensive root cause analysis to ensure the continuous uptime of our applications. This involves determining the exact location of the error, understanding the routing of the error, and examining the interactions between upstream and downstream applications in the multiservice architecture. By gaining a profound understanding of the application's flow, we can provide a thorough root cause analysis to present to the team, thereby safeguarding our applications from potential harm caused by these errors.

Efforts in this task play a pivotal role in maintaining the reliability and performance of our applications, ensuring that the DevOps team can respond promptly and effectively to any issues that arise. Through meticulous analysis and documentation, we can optimize the overall performance and reliability of our systems, enabling the company to deliver top-notch services to its users.
