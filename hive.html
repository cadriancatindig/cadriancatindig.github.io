Hive

Project Link: GitHub Repo

Project Demo: YouTube Demo

Hive is an impressive addition to Carlos Adrian's and his thesis group's portfolio, showcasing a multi-tenant Software as a Service (SaaS) platform with a powerful AI-driven Order Processing System designed to revolutionize wholesale businesses. Through a subscription-based model, wholesalers gain access to a comprehensive set of tools to efficiently manage their inventory, retailers, and orders.

One of the standout features of Hive is its incorporation of the XGBoost Machine Learning algorithm, which empowers wholesalers with the ability to predict the demand for each of their products accurately. This predictive capability offers valuable insights for better decision-making and inventory optimization, ultimately leading to improved business efficiency.

Moreover, Hive takes the wholesaler-retailer relationship to new heights with its innovative e-commerce/order portal. Retailers can seamlessly place their orders through this portal, streamlining the entire ordering process and facilitating smooth transactions.

By creating Hive, Carlos Adrian and his thesis groupmates has demonstrated a deep understanding of SaaS architecture, AI integration, and the potential for cutting-edge technologies to drive transformation in the wholesale sector. Through this project, Carlos Adrian aims to showcase their expertise in delivering robust, AI-powered solutions that empower businesses to thrive in a rapidly evolving marketplace.

Features and Functionalities
Features Functionalities
1. Authentication It requires the wholesaler and retailer to login into the system.
2. Authorization Each user role (wholesaler/retailer) has its own different view of the system.
3. Customized Web Color Scheme Each wholesaler will have their own preferred color scheme of the web application.
4. Retailer Account Management Wholesalers may register an account for their customer. The account may be deactivated or reactivated.
5. Demand Forecasting with Machine Learning It enables the system to forecast the product demand.
6. Order Creation Portal Allows the retailers to view products and send orders to the wholesale business.
7. Dashboard Allows wholesale businesses and retailers to get quick information about their orders according to its status.
8. Order Management Real-time recording orders are gathered from client order data and stored in a database. Updating the order status to give order updates to the retailer.
9. Inventory Management Allows the wholesale business to manage their inventory by adding, updating, and removing products from their inventory and add variation for each product if applicable.
10. Transaction History Payment transactions are recorded.
11. Sales Report Generate a sales report of the orders from the last thirty days.
12. Payment Gateway API The Stripe API will allow the system to process payments done via Credit/Debit Card.
