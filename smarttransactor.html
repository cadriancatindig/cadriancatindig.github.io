Smart Transactor

Project Link: GitHub Repo

Transaction Predictor App, is designed to assist bank employees in forecasting their customers' total transaction amounts for the upcoming half of the month. Leveraging the power of the Gradient Boosting Machine Learning model, this innovative app provides valuable insights into customer behavior, enabling the bank's employees to better understand and anticipate their clients' financial patterns. The Transaction Predictor App stands as a testament to Carlos Adrian's expertise in machine learning and its practical application in the banking industry. By accurately predicting transaction amounts, the app empowers bank employees to make data-driven decisions, enhance customer service, and optimize financial planning strategies.
