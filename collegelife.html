My College Life

My College Life has been a remarkable journey filled with a myriad of experiences, friendships, and invaluable life lessons. In the realm of learning, I had the privilege of delving into the vital domains of information technology, encompassing programming, networking, web design, software testing, and much more. These foundational courses proved to be the bedrock upon which I built my academic prowess, allowing me to excel in crucial tasks, including our thesis and daily work responsibilities. Guided by dedicated professors and fueled by my unwavering work ethic, I proudly attained academic excellence, securing a place on the dean's list for two consecutive years. These accomplishments not only bolstered my confidence but also reinforced my commitment to continuous growth and achievement. As I reflect on my College Life, I am filled with gratitude for the learning opportunities that have shaped my skills, knowledge, and prospects. With newfound insights, I eagerly embrace the exciting path that lies ahead, ready to apply what I have learned to make a meaningful impact on the world around me.

"Make Friends Who Force You To Level Up" - @BusinessMindset101

Throughout my college years, I forged friendships that have stood the test of time. These amazing individuals were not just my groupmates in various subjects; they became my pillars of support through life's ups and downs, fostering an environment of mutual growth and knowledge-sharing. Together, we created cherished memories, often venturing to unexplored places right after school, making the most of our time together.

I distinctly recall how we navigated the bustling streets of Manila using public transport, discovering the vibrant cityscape hand in hand. Those were the carefree days before the pandemic, where every experience was a lesson. However, as the pandemic struck, it didn't deter our connection. Instead, we adapted and found new ways to collaborate effectively through video conferencing apps like Zoom, Google Meet, and Discord.

Even in the face of adversity, we continued to support each other and overcome challenges, leveraging technology to work on group projects and access online resources provided by our professors. Together, we conquered the uncertainties of remote learning and emerged stronger as a cohesive unit.

I realize that my friendships and the experiences we shared have played an instrumental role in shaping my growth, both personally and academically. As I step into the future, I carry with me not only the knowledge gained from my courses but also the lessons learned from the power of camaraderie, resilience, and adaptability.

“You always pass failure on your way to success.” -Mickey Rooney.

Undoubtedly, one of the most profound lessons I gained during my college life was learning from failure. While I experienced numerous successes, such as consistently making it to the dean's list and achieving perfect scores, the moment that impacted me the most was the setback we encountered during our thesis.

Failing to pass our thesis left us feeling disheartened, and it seemed as though the world had come crashing down. Our academic motivation waned, and even the possibility of achieving the dean's list again felt distant. However, this pivotal moment sparked a crucial period of reflection for me and my groupmates. We gathered to analyze our weaknesses, identified areas for improvement, and devised a plan to secure a passing score for our thesis.

With a newfound determination, we set our sights on graduation and took each step with focused intent. Slowly but steadily, we made progress, overcoming challenges, and ultimately succeeded in completing our thesis project with a passing score. I firmly believe that this triumph wouldn't have been possible without the invaluable guidance of our thesis advisers and the support of our panelists, who played a crucial role in our journey to success.

Looking back, I am immensely grateful for the experience of failure. It taught me that setbacks are opportunities for growth and improvement. Embracing failure allowed me to strive for more and avoid complacency. I discovered the strength of perseverance and resilience, qualities that will undoubtedly serve me well in my future endeavors.

As I journey beyond college, I carry with me not only the knowledge I acquired but also the invaluable wisdom that failure, far from being a roadblock, is a stepping stone to becoming a better version of myself. I am thankful for this transformative experience, for it has shaped me into a more determined, resilient, and appreciative individual.

“The best teacher in life is experience” -LeBron James.

As I delve into the memories of my internship or practicum journey, I can't help but acknowledge the initial challenges I faced in securing a company for on-the-job training. Being a novice in the process of finding the right opportunity, it felt like an uphill battle. However, with relentless determination, I managed to find a company that welcomed me into their fold, providing me with a platform to acquire invaluable learnings and experiences.

This journey proved to be transformative, especially in the realm of the IT Industry, where I delved into the intricacies of DevOps and Software Engineering. Immersed in a dynamic environment, I gained hands-on experience, witnessing firsthand how theoretical knowledge converges with real-world practices. The technical skills I cultivated during this period have undoubtedly bolstered my confidence in the field.

Moreover, beyond technical expertise, my internship journey also emphasized the significance of soft skills. Working alongside professionals in a collaborative setting, I honed my communication, teamwork, and adaptability. These essential qualities not only made me more effective as a team member but also laid the groundwork for my future professional interactions.

I express heartfelt gratitude for this remarkable journey, as it has broadened my horizons and shaped my understanding of the IT industry. Through the challenges and triumphs, I have emerged as a more resilient and capable individual, ready to take on new challenges in my career. As I move forward, I carry with me the lessons learned during this internship, confident that they will continue to serve as a guiding light on my path to success.
